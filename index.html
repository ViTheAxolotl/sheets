Pasc

The continent of Pasc is vast filled with many different people and wildlife. Magic runs rampant around the continent, with strange tech from long ago loaming over everyone. Some believe who can control the tech will control the world.

Celiso

The town of Celiso was an upcoming town with it's fast culture and innovation, with the design of oil lamps. The whole town glowed during the nights, singling adventures who wish for a safe place to stay the night.
